# DEADMAN ANNIHILATION

## **BRACKET 1 — FINAL OPTIMIZED ROUTE**

**Combat 3 → ~50**

### **Core priorities (in order)**

1. **Buy Deft Strikes sigil (Gives accuracy boost across the board)**

2. Early Mage → Prayer

3. Early Litheness (Allows player to use any agility shortcut without needing the requirements) \



## **STEP 0 — LOGIN (0–2 MIN)**

On first login you have:

- Starter mage

- Early-game cape

- 12 tuna (inv), 30 tuna (bank)

- Chronicle + 10 charges

- Looting bag

- Dramen staff

- **Some starting skull points**

- **Get Menacing Mage**


## **STEP 1 — BUY SIGIL IMMEDIATELY (DO THIS FIRST)**

### **Go straight to Sigil Shop**

### **Buy:**

### **Sigil of Deft Strikes — 100 skull points**

- +30% accuracy vs NPCs

- Affects **all combat styles**

- This makes *every single action after faster \
*

⚠️ Do **not** train anything before buying this.

Time: **2–3 minutes**


## **STEP 2 — MAGE TRAINING & STRONGHOLD OF SECURITY**

### **Goal:**

- **5 Mage**** minimum**

- Generate early skull points efficiently

- Get 10k to split between 2 players

### **Where:**

- Rats / goblins / guards (safe areas)

- Barbarian village

### **How:**

- Eat tuna only when needed

Expected:

- Mage: 5-30

- HP: ~20–25

- Skull points from levels

⏱️ Time: **10-15 minutes**


## **STEP 3 — PRAYER SETUP (PROTECTION + POINTS)**


### **Lava Dragons Dragons → Chaos Altar**

1. Teleport to home

2. Get water staff, air runes, and mind runes

3. Teleport using chronicle to varrock

4. Run to **Wilderness Lava Dragon spot**

5. Kill lava dragons with water strike

6. Loot **bones in inventory **all other in looting bag

7. Run to chaos altar

8. Use bones (make sure you have enough to hit AT LEAST 43)

⏱️ Time: **20–30 minutes**


## **STEP 4 — SECOND SIGIL PURCHASE**

At this point you should have **~250–400 skull points total**.

### **Buy next:**

### **Sigil of Litheness — 250 pts**

- Passive Agility XP starts now

- Unlocks all shortcuts

- Massive long-term value


## **STEP 5 — FIGHT ARENA (FAST & SAFE)**

You now have:

- Deft Strikes

- Protect Melee

- Higher HP

This makes Fight Arena trivial.

### **How:**

1. Chronicle → Varrock

2. Run to **GE**

3. Use **Spirit Tree → Battlefield of Khazard**

4. Complete Fight Arena** \
**


### **Post-quest stats:**

- Attack: **~51**

- HP: ~30+

- Minimal food used

⏱️ Time: **15 minutes**
